Privacy Policy for Dice Roller

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Dice Roller ("the App") is developed and maintained by Asad Apps ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Dice Roller does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All app data including roll history, statistics, and user preferences are stored locally on your device and are never sent to our servers or any third party by us.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob is a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC. When you use the App, AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • IP address - used to estimate the general location of your device
  • Device and account identifiers - such as Android Advertising ID and app set ID
  • User product interactions - such as app launches, taps, and video views
  • Diagnostic information - such as crash logs, app launch time, hang rate, and energy usage

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Advertising and Interest-Based Ads

AdMob may use the information collected to serve personalized (interest-based) or non-personalized advertisements. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Go to Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

For more information about how Google uses data from apps that use their services, visit How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The App itself does not use cookies. However, third-party services such as Google AdMob may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies to serve and measure ads.

5. Data Security

All data transmitted by third-party services integrated in the App is encrypted using TLS (Transport Layer Security). Your locally stored app data remains on your device and is protected by your device's security measures.

6. Children's Privacy

Dice Roller is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is rated for general audiences and complies with Google Play's Families Policy requirements. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Data Retention

Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not retain any user data on our servers. Locally stored data on your device (roll history, statistics, preferences) can be cleared at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Reset your advertising identifier at any time
  • Delete all locally stored app data by clearing app data or uninstalling

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.
